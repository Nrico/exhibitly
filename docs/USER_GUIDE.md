## Getting Started

### Sign Up
Visit the login page and toggle to "Sign Up". Enter your email, password, and choose a unique **username**. Your username will determine your portfolio URL (e.g., `exhibitly.app/yourname`).

### Onboarding
Upon first login, you'll see an **Onboarding Checklist** on your dashboard. Follow these steps to get your site ready:
1.  **Complete Profile**: Add your full name and bio.
2.  **Upload Art**: Add your first artwork.
3.  **Customize Design**: Choose a theme.
4.  **Publish**: Your site is live immediately!

## Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your command center.
- **Stats**: View total site views, artwork counts, and sales status.
- **Recent Uploads**: Quickly see your latest added works.
- **Quick Actions**: "Upload New Work" button for fast access.

## Managing Artworks

Navigate to the **Inventory** tab to manage your collection.

### Uploading Art
1.  Click **Upload New Work** or drag-and-drop images onto the "Batch Upload" zone.
2.  You can drop multiple images at once to create multiple draft entries.
3.  Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP.

### Editing Details
Click on any artwork in the list to open the **Editor Panel**.
- **Title & Medium**: Essential details.
- **Status**:
    - `Available`: Visible on your site, "Inquire" button active.
    - `Sold`: Visible but marked as sold.
    - `Draft`: Hidden from the public site.
- **Price**: Optional.
- **Collection**: Group artworks by series (e.g., "Summer 2024", "Oil Paintings").
- **Notify Subscribers**: Check this box when publishing new work to email your subscribers.

### Reordering
Drag and drop artworks in the list using the **handle icon** (six dots) on the left of each row. The new order is automatically saved and reflected on your public site.

## Site Design & Themes

Navigate to the **Site Design** tab to customize your portfolio's look.

### Themes
Exhibitly offers three distinct, premium themes:
1.  **The White Cube** (Default): Minimalist, clean, masonry layout. Perfect for contemporary art.
2.  **The Archive**: Structured, grid-based, grey-box aesthetic. Ideal for photography and catalogs.
3.  **The Cinema**: Dark mode, split-screen layout. Great for dramatic, high-contrast work.

### Customization
- **Preview**: Toggle between "Mobile" and "Desktop" views to see how your site looks on different devices.
- **Publish**: Click "Publish Changes" to apply the selected theme to your live site.

## Gallery Features

If you have a **Gallery Account**, you have access to additional tools in the sidebar.

### Roster Management
Navigate to **Roster** to manage your represented artists.
- **Add Artist**: Create profiles for artists you represent (Name, Bio, Photo).
- **Public View**: Your site will have an "Artists" page listing your roster.

### Exhibitions
Navigate to **Exhibitions** to curate digital shows.
- **Create Exhibition**: Set a title, dates, and cover image.
- **Curate**: Select artworks from your inventory to include in the show. Drag to reorder them.
- **Publish**: Make the exhibition live on your "Exhibitions" page.

## Settings & Billing

Navigate to **Settings** to manage your account.

### Profile
- **Avatar**: Upload a profile picture.
- **Bio**: Update your short and long biographies.
- **Contact Info**: Add email, phone, and social media links.

### Data Export
- **Export CSV**: Download a spreadsheet of your inventory.
- **Export Site**: Download a ZIP file containing your entire site as static HTML and images (useful for backups).

### Billing
- **Manage Subscription**: Opens the Stripe Customer Portal to update payment methods or cancel your subscription.
